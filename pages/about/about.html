OUR STORY

2000, Origin

Boba Bar began as a small Father & Daughter business. With years of experience in the international restaurant business, Kenny (father), saw an opportunity in San Diego to provide a high quality beverage and dessert. Kenny pitched the idea of opening a boba shop to his daughter,

2013, Grand opening

Tiffany, who at the time was a college student at USD and milk tea was her go to caffeine of choice. Together with Kenny’s restaurant experience and Tiffany’s knowledge of boba, Boba Bar and Desserts was created in 2013.

2023, 2 New Boba Bar and Desserts

Fast forward to 2023, the family has opened an additional 2 locations serving a wide variety of drinks and desserts.
